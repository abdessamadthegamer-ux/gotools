How to Build a Rustic Wooden Shelf for Under $50

March 2025 8 min read Woodworking
Create beautiful, functional storage with this beginner-friendly guide to building a rustic wooden shelf using reclaimed materials and basic tools.

With smart shopping and reclaimed materials, you can create a beautiful shelf for a fraction of store-bought prices!

Key Steps

  • Source reclaimed wood: Check local listings or construction sites
  • Measure and cut: Precision is key for stability
  • Sand thoroughly: Create a smooth, splinter-free surface
  • Stain or paint: Protect and beautify your wood
  • Secure mounting: Ensure shelves are safely installed
